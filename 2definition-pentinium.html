Pentium

  1. Home
  2. Definitions
  3. Pentium
Description

  Pentium is made by Intel which is a CPU for a PC and workplaces that started being made in 1993.
  They are used for general uses like web browsing, word processing, videos, and even light gaming. Pentium processors are very affordable and reliable.
  The first model in 1993 consisted of 2 processors and 3.3 million transistors all in one chip. Since 1993, there have been many models made which include: Pentium Pro, Pentium II, Pentium III, Pentium 4, Pentium M, Pentium Dual-Core, Pentium D, and Pentium Gold. Each model is better in performance, speed, and efficiency.
