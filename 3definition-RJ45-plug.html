RJ45 Plug

  1. Home
  2. Definitions
  3. RJ45 Plug
Definition

  An RJ45 plug is an ethernet connector that is a standard connector which uses Ethernet cables. It is used to connect devices like computers or game consoles to either a router or wall socket that have Ethernet connections.
  An RJ45 plug has 8 pins which are in a rectangular shape. This goes into a RJ45 jack (AKA female connector) that is in electronical devices or wall outlets. It transmits high speed data signals by its reliable gold-plated connectors and cables. There are many different kinds of RJ45 plugs like 10BASE-T, 100BASE-TX, and 1000BASE-T.
